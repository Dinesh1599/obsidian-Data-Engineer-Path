###  The full logical order:

1. **FROM**
    
2. **ON**
    
3. **JOIN**
    
4. **WHERE**
    
5. **GROUP BY**
    
6. **HAVING**
    
7. **WINDOW FUNCTIONS** (`OVER`)
    
8. **SELECT**
    
9. **DISTINCT**
    
10. **ORDER BY**
    
11. **LIMIT / OFFSET / FETCH**
    

(Some systems may merge steps internally, but this is the correct _logical_ order.)
