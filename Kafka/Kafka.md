**Apache Kafka** is a **distributed event-streaming platform** designed to **publish, store, and process streams of records in real time**, enabling **high-throughput, fault-tolerant, and scalable data pipelines** between systems.

Think of Kafka as a chain reaction enabler. Where one event triggers another or a Real-time analytics.
